The artist

Circles of calm, made by hand

Hi, I'm Jagruti. Every mandala I make begins from a single centre point and grows outward — a small meditation in line, dot, and symmetry. No two are ever quite the same.

I create original works for sale, take on custom commissions for gifts and special moments, and share the whole process on video so you can watch the calm unfold.
